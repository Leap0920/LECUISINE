BICOL EXPRESS

Bicol Express, a renowned Filipino dish hailing from the Bicol region, is a culinary gem celebrated for its bold flavors and spiciness. This delectable creation features succulent pork belly slices, marinated in piquant shrimp paste, lending the meat a distinctive and savory kick. Bathed in a rich coconut milk bath, the dish achieves a creamy consistency that beautifully balances the intense heat, resulting in a harmonious flavor profile. The aromatic combination of minced garlic, onions, and fiery red chili peppers adds layers of complexity to the dish, elevating it to a gastronomic delight. Slow-cooked to perfection, the pork becomes tender, absorbing the robust flavors of the coconut-infused sauce. With the addition of green bell peppers offering a touch of mild sweetness, Bicol Express creates a culinary symphony that captivates the palate.

DIRECTIONS

1. In a bowl, combine sliced pork belly and shrimp paste. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.

2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté minced garlic until golden brown.

3. Add the marinated pork belly and cook until it turns brown.

4. Pour in coconut milk and add water. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for 20 minutes.

5. Add sliced onions, chopped red chili peppers, and green bell peppers. Stir and let it simmer for an additional 10 minutes.

6. Season with salt and pepper according to taste.

7. Continue simmering until the sauce thickens and the pork becomes tender.

8. Once done, transfer to a serving plate and serve hot with steamed rice.

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g Pork Belly, sliced

  • 3 tablespoons Shrimp Paste (Bagoong)

  • 1 cup Coconut Milk

  • 2 tablespoons Vegetable Oil

  • 5 cloves Garlic, minced

  • 1 large Onion, sliced

  • 5 pieces Red Chili Peppers, chopped (adjust according to spice preference)

  • Salt and Pepper to taste

  • 2 pieces Green Bell Pepper, sliced

  • 1 cup Water
