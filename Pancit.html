PANCIT CANTON

Pancit is a popular Filipino noodle dish known for its versatility and rich flavor. It is often served on special occasions and celebrations, symbolizing long life and good luck. The dish typically features a mix of stir-fried noodles, meat, and an assortment of vegetables, creating a harmonious blend of textures and tastes.

DIRECTIONS

1. Soak the chosen noodles in water until softened, then drain.

2. In a wok or large pan, sauté garlic and onions until aromatic.

3. Add the sliced meat and cook until browned.

4. Incorporate the vegetables, stir-frying until they are tender yet still crisp.

5. Push the ingredients to the side of the pan and scramble the eggs until cooked.

6. Mix the eggs with the rest of the ingredients and add the soaked noodles.

7. Pour soy sauce and/or fish sauce over the mixture, adjusting to taste.

8. Toss the noodles until well-coated and evenly cooked.

9. Serve hot and enjoy this delightful and symbolic Filipino dish!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pack ofBihon

  • 2 poundsChicken

  • wholeGarlic, minced

  • wholeOnion, chopped

  • 3 tbsp. Soy Sauce

  • 1 cupCarrot

  • 1 cupCabbage

  • 1 cupGreen Beans

  • ¼ cupBell Pepper
