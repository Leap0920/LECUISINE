GINATAANG HALO HALO

Ginataang Halo-Halo is a Filipino dessert renowned for its rich and creamy coconut milk base. This delightful concoction features a medley of colorful and textured ingredients, including sticky rice, yam, taro, jackfruit, banana, sweet potato, and tapioca pearls. Slowly simmered in a pot, these ingredients absorb the velvety goodness of coconut milk, creating a harmonious blend of sweet and savory flavors. To enhance the indulgence, evaporated and condensed milk are added, imparting a luscious sweetness to the dish. The result is a comforting and satisfying dessert that encapsulates the diverse tastes and textures of Filipino cuisine in every spoonful.

DIRECTIONS

1. In a large pot, combine sticky rice, yam, taro, jackfruit, banana, and sweet potato.

2. Pour coconut milk into the pot and bring to a simmer over medium heat.

3. Once the mixture is simmering, add tapioca pearls and continue to cook until the pearls are translucent.

4. Pour in evaporated milk and condensed milk, stirring gently to combine.

5. Add sliced cooking banana and continue to simmer until all the fruits and root vegetables are tender.

6. Adjust the sweetness by adding more condensed milk if desired.

7. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious Ginataang Halo-Halo!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Sticky Rice

  • 1 cup Yam (Ube), peeled and cubed

  • 1 cup Taro (Gabi), peeled and cubed

  • 1 cup Jackfruit (Langka), ripe and sliced

  • 1 cup Banana (Saba), sliced

  • 1 cup Sweet Potato (Kamote), peeled and cubed

  • 1 cup Tapioca Pearls

  • 1 can (400 ml) Coconut Milk

  • 1 cup Evaporated Milk

  • 1 cup Condensed Milk

  • 1 cup Cooking Banana (Saba), sliced
