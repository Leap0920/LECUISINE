COCONUT MACAROONS

Coconut macaroons are delightful, bite-sized treats known for their irresistible combination of chewy texture and sweet, coconut flavor. These confections typically consist of shredded coconut, sweetened condensed milk, and egg whites, creating a moist and dense consistency. Baked to golden perfection, coconut macaroons often feature a crispy exterior that gives way to a soft, gooey center. Their simplicity in ingredients and preparation makes them a popular choice for home bakers seeking a quick and delicious dessert. Whether enjoyed plain or dipped in chocolate, coconut macaroons are a timeless and satisfying indulgence.

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded coconut, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla extract. Mix well.

3. In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites with salt until stiff peaks form.

4. Gently fold the beaten egg whites into the coconut mixture until well combined.

5. Using a spoon or cookie scoop, drop rounded mounds of the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each macaroon.

6. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the edges of the macaroons are golden brown.

7. Allow the macaroons to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

8. Optional: Drizzle melted chocolate over the cooled macaroons for added flavor.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups Shredded Coconut

  • 3/4 cup Sweetened Condensed Milk

  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

  • 2 large Egg Whites, lightly beaten

  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
