GINISANG MUNGGO

Ginisang munggo is a Filipino dish made with sautéed mung beans, garlic, onions, and various ingredients like pork, shrimp, or fish. It's often seasoned with fish sauce or soy sauce and typically includes leafy greens such as spinach or malunggay. The dish has a hearty and savory flavor, and it's a popular comfort food in Filipino cuisine.

DIRECTIONS

1. In a pot, combine mung beans and water.

2. In a separate pan, heat oil over medium heat. Sauté onions until translucent, then add garlic and cook until golden brown.

3. Add tomatoes and cook until they are soft and mashed.

4. Pour the cooked mung beans into the pan and stir well.

5. Dissolve the Knorr cube in a little water and add it to the mixture.

6. Season with salt, pepper, and fish sauce according to taste. Mix thoroughly.

7. If using, add malunggay and spinach leaves. Stir until wilted.

8. Simmer for a few more minutes until all flavors meld.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Mung Beans

  • 2 cups Water

  • 2 tbsp. Cooking Oil

  • 1 large Onion

  • 3 cloves Garlic

  • 1 piece Tomato

  • 1 cup Malunggay Leaves

  • 1 cup Spinach Leaves

  • Salt to taste

  • Pepper to taste
