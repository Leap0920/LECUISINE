CASSAVA CAKE

Cassava cake is a delectable Filipino dessert renowned for its rich and moist texture. This sweet treat features freshly grated cassava, combined with a luscious blend of coconut milk, condensed milk, and evaporated milk, resulting in a harmonious marriage of flavors. The addition of melted butter, eggs, and a touch of vanilla extract contributes to its indulgent taste. Baked to perfection, the cassava cake emerges with a golden-brown top, enticing aroma, and a satisfyingly chewy consistency. Often served in squares or rectangles, this dessert is a delightful showcase of the Philippines' culinary creativity and love for sweet, comforting delicacies.

DIRECTIONS

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a baking pan with parchment paper.

2. In a large bowl, combine the grated cassava, coconut milk, condensed milk, evaporated milk, melted butter, and sugar. Mix well.

3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing thoroughly after each addition. Then, add the vanilla extract and salt. Mix until smooth.

4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top with a spatula.

5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-60 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

6. Allow the cassava cake to cool in the pan before slicing into squares or rectangles.

7. Serve and enjoy your delicious cassava cake!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups grated Cassava

  • 1 can (400ml) Coconut Milk

  • 1 can (300ml) Condensed Milk

  • 1 cup Evaporated Milk

  • 3/4 cup Butter, melted

  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar

  • 3 Eggs

  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
