KALDERETA

Kaldereta is a rich and flavorful Filipino stew known for its tender meat, often beef or goat, cooked in a savory tomato-based sauce. It typically includes potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, and sometimes olives, creating a hearty and delicious dish with a perfect blend of sweet and savory flavors.

DIRECTIONS

1. In a large pot, heat cooking oil and sauté onions and garlic until aromatic.

2. Add the beef (or goat) chunks and brown them on all sides.

3. Pour in the tomato sauce, liver spread, and beef broth.
Bring it to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the meat is tender.
This may take 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on the meat used.

4. Once the meat is tender, add potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers.
Continue simmering until the vegetables are cooked.

5. Add olives if desired, then season with salt and pepper to taste.

6. Stir in the grated cheese and let it melt into the stew, creating a creamy texture.

7. Adjust the seasoning if needed and simmer for a few more minutes until everything is well-incorporated.

8. Serve hot with steamed rice.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1.5 kg Beef

  • 3 tablespoon Cooking Oil

  • 1 finely chopped Onion

  • 1 cup Tomato Sauce

  • 1 cup Liver Spread

  • 1 cup Green Olives

  • 4 cloves minced Garlic

  • 1 cup Grated Cheese

  • 2 large peeled and slice Potatoes

  • 2 large peeled and slice Carrots

  • 2 sliced Red Bell Peppers

  • 2 sliced Green Bell Peppers

  • 2 cups Beef Broth
